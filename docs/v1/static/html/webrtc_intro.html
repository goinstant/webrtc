The Audio and Video widget provides a drop-in, back-end free video conferencing solution for your app. It's powered by GoRTC library, our integration with WebRTC. Using the Video and Audio widget means you don't need to setup a backend/server or configure WebRTC, just add our code into your app and you're set.

Try the Demo

If you want additional functionality beyond what's provided in the Video and Audio widget, you should look at the GoRTC library.
